# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Add Privacy Policy and Disclaimer pages to the Hostel Addas application with footer navigation links.

**Planned changes:**
- Create Privacy page component displaying comprehensive privacy policy content
- Create Disclaimer page component displaying legal disclaimers and terms
- Add routes for /privacy and /disclaimer pages
- Add Privacy and Disclaimer links to the footer navigation

**User-visible outcome:** Users can access Privacy Policy and Disclaimer pages through footer links, providing transparency about data handling and legal terms.
